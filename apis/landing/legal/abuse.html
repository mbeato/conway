DMCA and Abuse Reporting

Effective: April 2026

TL;DR

  • Report abuse or DMCA issues to abuse@apimesh.xyz
  • We respond to all reports within 48 hours
  • Include the API endpoint, timestamps, and description of the issue
  • Repeat offenders are permanently banned

1. Reporting Abuse

If you believe APIMesh APIs are being used to cause harm, please send a report to abuse@apimesh.xyz with the following information:

  • Your contact information (name and email)
  • Description of the abuse
  • API endpoint involved (if known)
  • Timestamps of the abusive activity
  • Any supporting evidence (logs, screenshots)

2. DMCA Takedown

If you believe APIMesh APIs are being used to infringe on your copyright, send a DMCA notice to abuse@apimesh.xyz including:

  • Identification of the copyrighted work being infringed
  • Identification of the infringing activity and the API endpoint involved
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement that the information in the notice is accurate, under penalty of perjury
  • Your physical or electronic signature

3. Response Timeline

  • Acknowledgment: Within 48 hours of receiving a report
  • Investigation: Completed within 5 business days
  • Notification: Reporter is informed of the outcome and actions taken

4. Counter-Notice

If you receive a DMCA notice and believe it was sent in error, you may submit a counter-notice to abuse@apimesh.xyz including:

  • Identification of the material that was removed or disabled
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed by mistake
  • Your name, address, and phone number
  • Consent to jurisdiction of your local federal court
  • Your physical or electronic signature

5. Enforcement

  • First offense: Warning via email with details of the violation
  • Second offense: 7-day account suspension
  • Third offense: Permanent account ban
  • Severe violations: Immediate permanent ban (DDoS, illegal activity, repeated DMCA infringement)

6. Contact

Abuse reports and DMCA notices: abuse@apimesh.xyz

General support: support@apimesh.xyz
